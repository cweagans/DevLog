`ROLLBACK` is used with [[T-SQL Begin Transaction|s.q.tsql.syntax.tcl.begin-transaction]] to revert changes. If you wanted to keep changes made you would [[T-SQL Commit|s.q.tsql.syntax.tcl.commit]] them.
