## Pauses

For your slides to pause on say a list populating on the screen use pause to control it

```latex
	\begin{itemize}
		\item frame\pause
		\item beamer themes\pause
		\item pauses and slides\pause
		\item sections\pause
		\item images
		\item columns
	\end{itemize}
```
